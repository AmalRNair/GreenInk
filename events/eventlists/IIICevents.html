Mentorship at Irikkur Innovation &
Incubation Centre (IIIC)


In mid-September, an exciting announcement echoed through the community as IIIC unveiled its inaugural internship and mentorship program. Fresh engineering graduates were offered three months of free mentorship in disciplines ranging from Full stack web development to DevOps and entrepreneurship. A structured interview process meticulously selected a group of students aligned with the program's goals, setting the stage for a transformative journey.

The journey officially commenced with a grand kick-off meeting on October 11, 2023, at Hotel Samudra Auditorium. Key professionals, entrepreneurs, and enthusiasts from various sectors graced the occasion, setting the tone for IIIC's mission: to foster entrepreneurship, startups, and technological innovation. Spearheaded by visionary Adv. Sajeev Joseph MLA in collaboration with IT Head & Coordinator Vivek KC, the initiative aimed to bridge the gap between academia and industry.

Offline classes commenced promptly, with a dedicated cohort of full-time interns and campus interns from GCEK. Important discussions and content were meticulously recorded and shared via a dedicated YouTube channel. Weekends were abuzz with business-student connect programs, facilitating networking and learning opportunities.

As the journey progressed, a hybrid mode of classes was introduced, blending online and offline learning experiences to accommodate diverse preferences. Industrial visits to game development studios and MiZone provided invaluable insights and inspiration to participants, igniting a passion for careers in technology and entrepreneurship.

A memorable community workshop hosted by Dr. TPS, JNAANAM, brought together wisdom and insights from elderly attendees, enriching participants with invaluable life lessons and career advice. Despite a planned IT Park visit in Cochin being postponed, participants engaged in SWOT analysis, soft skills development, and hands-on projects, including the development of a food delivery app and startup incubation initiatives.

The outcomes spoke volumes: successful job placements, continued job finding support, and the nurturing of innovative ventures in various sectors. The journey of IIIC epitomized passion, expertise, and commitment, leaving an indelible mark on the landscape of technology and entrepreneurship. As convocation events were set to unfold in March 2024, the legacy of IIIC continued to inspire future generations of innovators and changemakers.
