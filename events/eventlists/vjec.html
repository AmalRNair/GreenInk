Mentorship at Vimal Jyothi
Engineering College

Vimal Jyothi Engineering College in Chemperi launched an impactful mentorship initiative led by Vivek KC and a team including Ramdev and Aromal, aiming to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry needs. The driving force behind this initiative was the collective commitment to give back to the society by guiding the budding talents of the college. Coordinated with precision by Donmariya in collaboration with the ACM Students Chapter, the program kicked off with an engaging webinar on DevOps and SRE, providing students with insights from industry experts and real-world problem-solving experiences.

The students were actively engaged in discussions and problem-solving exercises, creating a collaborative learning environment. Encouraging active participation and discussions, the mentorship program goes beyond technical proficiency, emphasizing soft skills and professional etiquette. The goal is to prepare students for diverse career paths, ensuring they thrive in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
