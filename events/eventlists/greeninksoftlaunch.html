Greenink.pro's Remarkable Soft Launch at MiZone Kannur -
Paving the Way for Innovation


On February 23, 2024, a significant milestone marked the beginning of a promising journey for Greenink.pro. The company's soft launch took place at Mizone Kannur - Malabar Innovation and Entrepreneurship Zone. This event not only unveiled Greenink.pro to the world but also allowed the founders to share their vision and ideas with esteemed individuals, including Kerala Startup Mission CEO Anoop Ambika, MiZone CEO, Chairman, and a diverse audience of professionals and experts.
The soft launch was a meticulously planned event, bringing together key figures in the startup ecosystem. The presence of Anoop Ambika, CEO of Kerala Startup Mission, added a touch of prestige to the occasion. The participation of MiZone's CEO and Chairman, along with a spectrum of professionals, created an environment rich in expertise and insights.

The founders of Greenink.pro seized the opportunity to articulate their vision and ideas during the event. A well-prepared presentation outlined the company's goals, mission, and how it aimed to make a significant impact in the tech and entrepreneurship landscape. The focus on innovation, mentorship, and holistic learning stood out as key pillars of Greenink.pro's vision.

The response from the audience was overwhelmingly positive. Professionals, including Anoop Ambika and other eminent personalities, expressed their appreciation for the company's innovative approach. The presentation resonated well with the attendees, showcasing the potential impact of Greenink.pro in the field of technology, mentorship, and skill development.

One of the major highlights of the soft launch was the expressed interest from the attendees,to collaborate with Greenink.pro. The event opened doors to potential partnerships and collaborations that could further enhance the company's reach and influence in the startup and innovation ecosystem.

Reflecting on the soft launch, the founders of Greenink.pro expressed their joy and satisfaction. The positive feedback and interest shown by industry leaders and professionals validated the efforts put into building the company. The event marked a successful and impactful introduction of Greenink.pro to the startup community.

The soft launch at MiZone Kannur was not just an event; it was the commencement of a great journey for Greenink.pro. The positive reception, expressions of interest for collaboration, and the presence of influential figures set the stage for the company's future endeavors. The founders are optimistic and look forward to leveraging the momentum gained during the soft launch to drive Greenink.pro towards greater success in the tech and startup landscape.

The soft launch at MiZone Kannur was a pivotal moment for Greenink.pro, providing a platform to showcase its vision, receive valuable feedback, and open doors for collaboration. As the company sets sail into the vast sea of opportunities, the soft launch acts as a beacon, guiding Greenink.pro towards a future filled with innovation, growth, and positive impact in the entrepreneurial world.
