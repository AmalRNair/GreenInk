Healthy Living



In the vision of a "Healthy New Year 2023," Claygreen, an organic farming company, aspires to cultivate a nation where health and happiness flourish. With a primary focus on cancer prevention, we tackle critical health concerns rooted in our food habits. Embracing contemporary lifestyle trends, Claygreen advocates prioritizing health for a brighter future. We believe that gold really is in the fields, and that soil is life!

Our goal is to empower everyone to grow and consume their own organic foods, or to source from trusted locations for those in need. Transitioning from Uma 30% bran to Lavanya 60% bran, Claygreen is on a committed path to going 100% for a healthier lifestyle. Collaborating with Mayyil Rice FPO, awarded FPO of the year 2022, we promote a healthier food and lifestyle paradigm.

We believe in bringing change in our homes first, ensuring the quality and trust of our products. Having validated the organic rice from Mayyil Rice Producers, Claygreen proudly distributed it to the people of Chirakkal, Kannur. Our vision extends to cultivating healthy homes, lands, and towns, fostering generations of happiness and health.

Claygreen's goal is to build strong families and relationships, fostering mental and physical fitness, beginning a transformative journey towards a healthier and happier life.
