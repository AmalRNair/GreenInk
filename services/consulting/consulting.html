Our Approach

For both Cloud DevOps and DevSecOps Consulting Services, we at CloudThat believe that a continuous approach is the finest way to do it. For example, testing is not done just at the final stage, but we prefer to take care of bugs as and when they are detected, from the very beginning itself. This way, there is less pressure and panic at the time of the deployment stage.

  • 01 Continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD)

    CI integrates code from developers into a shared repository for early debugging. CD quickens software automation and new feature releases.

  • 02 Continuous Testing (CT) & Deployment

    CT ensures automated testing at every stage of the development cycle. Deployment ensures automation of the entire release cycle.

  • 03 Continuous Monitoring

    This ensures that compliance issues and security risks are handled by forensics, threat intelligence, incident response and root cause analysis.

  • 04 Continuous Feedback & Operations

    Continuous feedback enables organizations to get reactions of end-users to fix critical issues. Operations minimizes the need for planned downtime.

Frequently Asked Questions

We have answered some frequently asked questions on Devops & DevSecOps Consulting Services.

  • What is DevSecOps and how does it relate to Consulting Services?

    DevSecOps is a methodology that integrates security practices early in the software development lifecycle. DevSecOps Consulting Services guide organizations in adopting this approach, ensuring security is seamlessly integrated into the development process.

  • What are the key components of DevSecOps Consulting?

    Key components of DevSecOps Consulting Services include security assessment, creating security policies and guidelines, implementing automation for security testing, integrating security tools, and providing training and education to development teams on security best practices.

  • What role does automation play in DevSecOps Consulting Services?

    Automation is a crucial aspect of DevSecOps Consulting Services, enabling continuous security testing, vulnerability scanning, compliance checks, and rapid response to security incidents, which ultimately enhance the overall security posture of the software development process.

  • How do I choose the right DevSecOps Services for my organization?

    When choosing DevSecOps Consulting Services, consider their experience in your industry, expertise in security practices, ability to integrate with your existing processes, success stories, and commitment to continuous improvement and learning.
