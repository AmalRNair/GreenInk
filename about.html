ABOUT US

WHAT IS GREENINK

Greenink is a dynamic organization to bridge the gap between academia and the IT industry. It offers subscription-based courses, mentorship programs, consulting services, and placements for students and young professionals. With a focus on industry-relevant skills and community engagement, Greenink prepares individuals for success in the IT sector while adapting to evolving industry trends.
The vision is to reduce the gap between the two sectors, equipping people with skills relevant to the industry, encouraging community involvement, and adjusting to changing industry trends, Greenink aims to revolutionise the IT industry and produce highly qualified and productive professionals who are prepared to succeed in their chosen fields.

HOW IT ALL STARTED

In the ever-evolving landscape of technology, my journey with Greenink began as a response to a critical issue that caught my attention. A glaring gap existed between what students were learning in academic institutions and what the dynamic IT industry demanded. Fueled by my own early challenges and a genuine desire to give back, I initiated a mentorship program on LinkedIn in 2020, aptly named "Claygreen buddy."

Initially, the mentorship program aimed to assist students in honing their skills and habits. With the guidance and support of MLA Adv. Sajeev Joseph, I got an opportunity to conduct a three-month internship program by IIIC (Irikkur Innovation and Incubation Center).However, as I delved deeper, it became apparent that short mentorships were insufficient. Recognizing the need for a more comprehensive approach, the program evolved from 6 months to a year. As the community grew, another challenge emerged – educators in schools and colleges lacked sufficient knowledge about real-world technology. This realization laid the foundation for Greenink.pro.
The vision driving Greenink.pro is clear – to reduce the divide between academia and industry, equipping individuals with skills aligned with industry needs, promoting community involvement, and adapting to evolving industry trends. The goal is nothing short of revolutionizing the IT industry and producing highly qualified professionals poised for success.

This journey began modestly in 2020 with the Claygreen buddy program. Over time, it became evident that teachers needed support as well. Thus, Greenink.pro expanded its horizons to offer online courses and startup advice. Collaborating with colleges, we provide hands-on training both online and in person, ensuring a holistic approach to education. From a small idea in 2020, Greenink.pro has blossomed into a significant force in 2024, making a tangible impact by helping individuals learn and succeed in tech jobs. In just three years, our alumni have achieved remarkable success in top companies, their careers soaring to new heights with multiplied benefits. Through dedication, guidance, and an unwavering commitment to excellence, Greenink has demonstrated that, with the right support, anyone can thrive in the tech world.
